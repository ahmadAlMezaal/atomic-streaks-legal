Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a new habit?

Tap the + button on the home screen. Give your habit a name, choose an identity it belongs to (like "Athlete" or "Reader"), and set your schedule. That's it‚Äîyou're ready to start building your streak!

What happens if I miss a day?

Your streak resets to zero, but your total completion history is preserved. Remember: one missed day doesn't erase your progress. The goal is consistency over time, not perfection.

How do I back up my data?

Go to Settings ‚Üí Data ‚Üí Export. This creates a backup file you can save or share. To restore, use Settings ‚Üí Data ‚Üí Import and select your backup file.

Is my data synced to the cloud?

No‚ÄîAtomic Streaks is built local-first. All your habits and streaks stay on your device. This means your data is private and the app works offline. Cloud sync is planned for a future update.

How do I change notification times?

Go to Settings ‚Üí Notifications to customize your Daily Briefing time (morning reminder) and Streak Rescue time (evening nudge for incomplete habits).

What are Identities?

Identities represent who you want to become‚Äîlike "Athlete," "Reader," or "Early Riser." Each habit is tied to an identity. This isn't just organization; it's psychology. You're not just tracking workouts, you're becoming an Athlete.

How do I delete my data?

Go to Settings ‚Üí Data ‚Üí Clear All Data. This permanently deletes all habits, streaks, and settings from your device. Since we don't store your data on servers, there's nothing to delete on our end.
