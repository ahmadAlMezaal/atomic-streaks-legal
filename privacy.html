Privacy Policy

Atomic Streaks

Last updated: December 29, 2024

The Short Version

Your habits are yours. All habit data stays on your device. We collect minimal analytics to improve the app, and that's it.

What Data Stays on Your Device

Atomic Streaks is built local-first. This means:

  • Your habits, streaks, and completion history are stored only on your device
  • Your identity labels and personal goals never leave your phone
  • Timer sessions and focus data remain completely private
  • No account or sign-up required to use the app

You can export your data anytime using the built-in backup feature. You own your data‚Äîwe never have access to it.

What We Collect

We use Mixpanel for anonymous analytics to understand how the app is used and improve it. This includes:

  • App opens and screen views
  • Feature usage (e.g., "user completed a habit", "user started a timer")
  • Device type and OS version
  • App version

This data is anonymized and cannot be linked back to you personally. We do not collect the names of your habits, your identity labels, or any content you create.

Push Notifications

If you enable notifications, we use Expo Push Notifications to send you reminders. This requires:

  • A device push token (a random identifier for your device)
  • Your notification preferences

Push tokens are used solely to deliver notifications you've requested. You can disable notifications anytime in your device settings.

What We Don't Do

  • We don't sell your data to anyone
  • We don't show you ads
  • We don't track your location
  • We don't access your contacts, photos, or other apps
  • We don't create advertising profiles

Data Security

Since your habit data is stored locally on your device, it's protected by your device's built-in security (passcode, Face ID, etc.). Analytics data transmitted to Mixpanel is encrypted in transit.

Children's Privacy

Atomic Streaks is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If we make significant changes to this policy, we'll update the "Last updated" date and notify users through the app.
